Definition

The statistical area level 1 (SA1) is a geographic area constructed from the entire grid block. SA1 is usually designed to be the smallest unit to publish census data. The population of SA1 is between 200 and 800, with an average population of about 400. SA1 is also used as a building block for many ASGS definition areas.[Abs.gov.au. (2018)]
